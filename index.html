Customer Service

We are here for you — always.

📦 How long does delivery take?

Within Lagos, delivery takes 45–60 minutes. Delays due to traffic will be communicated immediately via WhatsApp.

🔄 Can I modify or cancel my order?

You can cancel or change your order within 5 minutes of placement. After cooking has started, we cannot accept changes.

🌡️ Are meals kept hot on delivery?

Yes! All meals are packed in insulated thermal bags. Your food arrives hot and fresh, just like it left our kitchen.

💰 What is your refund policy?

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, we will replace it or offer a full refund within 24 hours of complaint.

🧑‍🍳 Are the meals cooked daily?

Absolutely. Every meal is cooked fresh on the day of order. We do not serve reheated or day-old food.

📱 How do I track my order?

After ordering, you'll receive a WhatsApp confirmation with a live tracking link so you can monitor your delivery in real time.
